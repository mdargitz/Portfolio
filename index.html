Frames

A Staff Scheduling App

Frames is a schedule management app aimed at helping shift managers keep track of their weeks! Users can manage employees, keep track of availability and avoid scheduling snafus.

Retail and restaurant managers are the most likely users of this app, especially those that have many employees and many hours to fill.

This app was built by a team of four developers, all wildly talented and skilled!

Guessmon

A Pokemon Guessing Game

Guessmon is a Pokemon memorization app that helps users learn their Pokemon and focus on only showing those Pokemon the user knows less.

Beyond feeding our love of all things cute and pocket-sized, we built this app with a spaced repetition algorithm using linked lists.

Any and all Pokemon fans are welcome to come test their skills! Users will be able to train forever, constantly see updated rates of success and catch 'em all!

Please take a moment to check out my incredible co-developer on this project, Terrance Corely.

Doodle Up

A Gamified Artist's Training App

If you enjoy playing games & making art, DoodleUp is the app for you. Incentivized practice, integrated tutorials & challenges await!

This app is built for the beginner and advanced artist alike. Users looking to develop new skills or continue building on the basics are invited to see what DoodleUp has to offer.

When I found I was playing more games than doodling in my sketchbook, I set out to find an app that would allow me to do both. Failing to find what I wanted, DoodleUp was born!



In order to try out DoodleUp, please download the Expo app to your mobile device.

Next click here and scan the QR code or send yourself the link to download the app and enjoy!

June 2018

Chronicle

A Writer's Aide

This web app is a writer's tool to help organize the characters, plots, settings and general information of your stories.

By providing a standardized means of input, Chronicle gives writers a birds-eye view of the where their world needs fleshing out and what can be improved.

Chronicle can serve anyone looking to put together a novel, campaign or other storytelling device.

See from a glace which aspects need more information and which ones are ready to be used!

Hey, I'm Marina!

As a student in Thinkful's Engineering Immersion program I feel as if I've been on vacation for the past five months.

Not only have I had the honor to work with some incredibly talented people, but I get to program real, usable applications every day; built from the ground up, using popular frameworks and solving real life problems.

I get to work with a range of people from different backgrounds, ideas and skill sets- resulting in not only a better product, but my own deeper understanding and personal engagement.

Web development is like a good mystery novel. Just as soon as you discover one answer, many new mysteries appear. How can I better identify this problem? How could software help my client reach their goals? Which libraries or frameworks will do the job best?

When I'm not glued to my computer, I enjoy borrowing my friends' dogs for loops around the dog park*, slaying a dragon or two in Skyrim and doodling with my Wacom tablet.

If you haven't already, please feel free to take a gander at my projects or to get in touch!
